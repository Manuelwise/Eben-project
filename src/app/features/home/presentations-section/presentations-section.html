Presentations - PalmX Thursdays

PalmX Thursday is a weekly online series held every Thursday at 6
PM GMT (or 18:00 GMT your time), where researchers in
neuroscience, AI, and robotics across Africa share their work,
spark discussions, and connect with others. It's a space for
learning, collaboration, and building confidence through open
presentations and community engagement.

5 minutes— Introduction

Brief introduction of the speaker and the topic. Set the context and outline the flow of the session.

35 minutes— Presentation

The speaker delivers their full presentation without interruptions. This allows for a smooth, uninterrupted explanation of their research or project.

20 minutes— Q&A and Discussion

After the presentation, the floor is opened for questions and discussion. The audience can seek clarifications, explore deeper ideas, and engage with the speaker in a relaxed, conversational style.
