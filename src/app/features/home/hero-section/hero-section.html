PalmX - Cultivating Minds Under the Palm: Neuroscience, Robotics, and Beyond.

PalmX Institute is an African-led platform dedicated to advancing neuroscience, neurotechnology, robotics, and AI. We create a space for genuine knowledge exchange, where research and innovation are rooted in both local context and global collaboration.

Through weekly webinars featuring research projects and the sharing of valuable resources, alongside carefully curated articles, PalmX fosters a dynamic platform for learning and connection. We bring together thinkers, researchers, and innovators to share insights and shape Africa's role in the future of science and technology.
